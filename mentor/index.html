Advice for Graduate Students

This page is a collection of links to useful and/or interesting material for how to succeed at grad school. Some of the material is replicated locally; the local copies are available only within Cornell University.

Life continues after grad school... Some information and advice on career decisions, the job search, and succeeding as a new professor. This page also gives information on networking with other CUCS "last-years".
