Position filled by: Merged with Colloquium Czar

The Videotaping Czars are responsible for videotaping all colloquia and job talks. These typically occur in the fall semester on Thursday afternoons, and in the spring semester on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons.

The colloquium schedule is at www.cs.cornell.edu/Events/colloquium.
