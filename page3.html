Lapsed Czarships

The following czarships are no longer active, due to lack of interest or judgment that they are no longer needed. If you would like to see one of these czarships reactivated, contact the Czar Czar.

Relocation Czar: The Relocation Czar helps incoming first-year students find transportation from the airport or bus station to their new residence or to a local hotel if they have not been able to find an apartment yet.

T-Shirt Czar: The T-Shirt Czar organizes creating a design for the department t-shirt (either by themself or via a contest) and getting the t-shirts made up and distributed to those who wish to purchase them.
